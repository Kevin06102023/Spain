Location and General Information

Spain is located in southwestern Europe on the Iberian Peninsula. It is bordered by Portugal to the west, France and Andorra to the northeast, and the Mediterranean Sea to the southeast.

The country has a population of approximately 47 million people and covers an area of about 505,990 square kilometers.

Spain is part of the continent of Europe and is a member of the European Union.
