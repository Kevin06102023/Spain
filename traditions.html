Spanish Traditions & Celebrations

Spain is known for its lively festivals, cultural celebrations, and unique traditions. Many of these reflect centuries-old customs and religious influences that continue to play an important role in modern Spanish life.

Every region in Spain has its own local events, but some festivals are celebrated nationwide.

Famous Spanish Traditions:

  • Las Fallas – A festival of fire held in Valencia
  • San Fermín – The Running of the Bulls in Pamplona
  • La Tomatina – Tomato throwing festival in Buñol
  • Semana Santa – Holy Week parades and processions
  • Día de los Reyes – Epiphany celebrated on January 6th
