About this Project

This website is a visual and interactive report about Spain. It was developed as part of an English assignment and aims to show the beauty, culture, and importance of this country. All sections are written in English and include rich content such as images, icons, and interactive elements.

The goal is to provide a complete experience: informative and visually pleasant, simulating a digital presentation or report format.
