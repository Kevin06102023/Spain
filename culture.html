Culture of Spain

Spain has a rich cultural heritage influenced by various civilizations such as the Romans, Moors, and Visigoths. Its culture is known worldwide for its passionate music, vibrant festivals, and traditional dances.

The Spanish language, or Castilian, is the official language and is spoken by the vast majority of the population.
